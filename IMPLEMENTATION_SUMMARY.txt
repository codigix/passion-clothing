  5. Quantity
  6. Amount
  7. Procurement Status
  8. Production Status

  10. Progress
  11. Delivery Date
  
- Optional (7 Columns):
  12. Order Date
  13. Advance Paid (₹) - Green

  15. Rate per Piece (₹)
  16. Created By

DEPARTMENT LAYOUTS

- Procurement: Procurement Status, Order Date, Qty
- Production: Production Status, Progress, Delivery
- Logistics: Status, Progress, Delivery, Amount
- Sales: Project, Customer, Status, Amount

AUTO-SAVE FEATURES
- Saves to: Browser localStorage
- Key: "salesDashboardVisibleColumns"
- Persists: Across page refreshes
- Scope: Per device/browser
